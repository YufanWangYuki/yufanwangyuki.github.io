Optic Cup and Disc Segmentation Based on Stereoscopic Fundus Images for Glaucoma Diagnosis

Independent Researcher

2019.03 - 2019.05 Literature review on the deep learning methods applied in glaucoma diagnosis.

2019.05 - 2019.07 Preprocessed the 6000+ stereoscopic fundus images, applied U-Net to segment optic disc and cup as well as do the job of fine tuning. The IoU is 0.75 which is not satisfied so I decided to use it as my benchmark.

2019.09 - 2019.09 Several papers researching on the field of stereoscopic images in Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2019 came out, which were pretty inspiring! By comparison, DAVA-Net is more suitable for my project, because the input and output of the model are both pictures, and the sub-network of it can extract the depth information in the stereo picture.

2019.09 - present Adjust the DAVA-Net structure to make it suitable for optic disc and cup segmentation.
